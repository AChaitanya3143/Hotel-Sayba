Our Story

Where Great Food Meets Great Company

Hotel Sayba is more than just a restaurant — it's where great food meets great company. Nestled in the heart of Kalwa, we've been serving the community with flavors that feel like home for over a decade.

Whether you're dining with family after a long day, celebrating with friends, or grabbing a quick bite, our kitchen crafts every dish with passion and care. From aromatic tandoor specialties to bold Indo-Chinese creations, every plate tells a story of taste and tradition.
